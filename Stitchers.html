About Us


Here at Stitchers, our initiative is to provide products to our customers which are hand-made using eco-friendly materials. Our products will not be for everyone. Our products are for people who look at the world in a preserving manner, people who want to do good today for a better tomorrow.


We want our customers to know how quality and long-lasting items can impact their satisfaction of spending their hard-earned money instead of watching their valuables deteriarate in value day-by-day. Our goal is to provide a vast range of eco-friendly products that can range from functional to non-functional. You can view more on our Campaign webpage about our plans for preserving the future and what we plan to provide to our customers by the year 2023.
