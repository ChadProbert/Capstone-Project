Recruitment Campaign



We're recruiting!


We want you to be apart of our intitiative. By the beginning of 2023, we are aiming to provide a vast amount of products to our customers which are eco-friendly and hand-crafted to perfection each time. The products can range from clothing; to household furniture; to animal products or even art creations. We want you to send us your ideas along with the process to creating the hand-crafted item.

If successfully selected, you will get to decide on the name for the product as well as recieve a job invitation to join the product design team at Stitchers situated in Johannesberg, South Africa. We will be selecting 25 people to come and join us! Submission deadlines close on the 20th of September 2021. Selected people will recieve their job invitations on the 1st of October 2021. We wish you the best!
